LINEAR REGRESSION ANALYSIS

Posted on 2023-04-12 Edited on 2023-04-14

My notes on the 2023 spring Linear Regression Analysis course taught by Professor Zaiying Zhou from Center for Statistical Science, Tsinghua University.

Lecture 1: Prelude

Goals of the Course

We will cover simple and multiple regression, one and two-way analysis of variance. The emphasis will be on

  1. conceptual understanding of the models
  2. analysis methods and their intuitions
  3. interpretation of results
  4. proper use of linear model software (R)

Mathematical manipulations are important but not the focus. Intuitive and statistical understanding of the theory is nonetheless required.
